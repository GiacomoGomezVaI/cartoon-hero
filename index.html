Cri-Kee

About Cri-Kee

Cri-Kee is a "lucky" cricket purchased by Grandmother Fa, who believed Mulan needed all the help she could receive. Cri-Kee goes through various perils and survives each of them, he does not speak and merely communicates through chirps, but he is understood by mythical creatures, such as Mushu.

Cri-Kee's Characteristics and friends

Cri-Kee's characteristics

  1. He's lucky
  2. He does strive to be useful and is therefore quick to aid those in need
  3. His guidance is taken for what it's worth.

Cri-Kee's friends

Cri-Kee's best mate is without a doubt Mushu the chinese dragon, togheter they do their best to help and protect Mulan.
